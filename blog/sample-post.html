Electrical Safety Basics for Homeowners

Always shut off power at the breaker before doing wiring work. Use proper insulated tools and follow local codes. When in doubt, hire a licensed electrician.

Tips

  • Replace damaged cords and worn outlets.
  • Install Ground Fault Circuit Interrupters (GFCIs) in kitchens and bathrooms.
  • Keep wiring away from moisture and high heat.
